Authentic Flavors of the Coromandel Coast

At Namma Chennai Kitchen, we bring the lively streets of Marina Beach and the rich heritage of Mylapore straight to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, utilizing stone-ground batters, hand-pounded masalas, and local Tamil culinary techniques.

Every tiffin is served with sambar and freshly churned chutneys. Every meal is an celebration of spice, texture, and hospitality. Welcome to a true feast of Madras.
